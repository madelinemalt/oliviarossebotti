Get to Know
The one who does it all.

Olivia is a Director & COO at Goldsmith & Co, the boutique retained search firm focused exclusively on asset management. She specializes in placing top talent at hedge funds, PE firms, VC shops, and beyond — with deep expertise in Chief of Staff, IR, and senior operational roles.

Before returning to Goldsmith, she spent time at Citadel in Business Development, bringing institutional knowledge from one of the world's most elite hedge funds. Before that, she rose through the ranks at H.W. Anderson, going from Associate Consultant to Senior Consultant in under three years, recruiting across equity long/short, quantitative, and commodity trading.

She's been in the game for 6+ years and brings the kind of energy that makes both candidates and clients feel like they're in the best hands possible.
